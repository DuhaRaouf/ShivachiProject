About

NOHA MAHMOUD - Fashion Designer

Her inspiration came from the Attitude to be Ambitious and still Enjoy life. The realization came early: Only a Combination brings Happiness.. The idea ame already as a teenager because as a Professional Tennis player she Traveled the World! During her countless trips it was Never possible to put all the belongings in only One bag which could be worn Comfortable and looked Chic at the same time. "In the phase of creation, in my designs”.


At SHIVACHI, We're more than a Brand, We're about You and Everything you Love.


We Are a company of Dreamers, Thinkers, Movers, Doers and Believers. We're not just Creating Designs, We Are Helping people to Express themselves. These are more than just pieces of fabric. They're Keepsakes that will remain with you for Years. With every garment we create, We are Sharing with you a piece of ourselves.
